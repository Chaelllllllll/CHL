Tutorial

  • Download Webvium in this link

To get your cookie

  • Open Webvium and navigate to the URL. Search for Facebook and open it.
  • Log in to your Facebook dummy account.
  • Click the three dots (â‹®) in the top right corner of Webvium to open the menu.
  • From the menu, select DevTools.
  • Once in DevTools, scroll up and click on Cookie Manager.
  • Copy your cookie.

To create your bot

  • Go to the Home tab.
  • Paste your cookie.
  • Select the commands you want your bot to have.
  • Choose the events.
  • Enter your prefix (optional).
  • Read the terms and conditions, check the checkbox, and submit.
